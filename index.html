About Genta

Genta was a productivity tool for people who work on solo projects. Oftentimes, we'd much rather switch from our myriad of Google Docs files, todo lists, and scattered planners to a proper project management tool. But proper tools are typically way too complex and not what we need. So, Genta is built from the ground up to offer one centralised place for all your notes and todos!
